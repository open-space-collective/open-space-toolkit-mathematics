\mbox{\hyperlink{classostk_1_1math_1_1curvefitting_1_1interp_1_1_cubic_spline}{Cubic\+Spline}}. 

\begin{DoxyVerb}                        A cubic Spline interpolator is a spline where each piece is a third-degree polynomial specified in Spline form, that is
                        by its values and first derivatives at the end points of the corresponding domain interval.
